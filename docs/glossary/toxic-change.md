---
id: toxic-change
title: Toxic change
summary: Change that links compartments or policies, creating long-term privacy leaks.
related: [change-output-hygiene]
keywords: [change, segregation]
---

Toxic change arises when a transaction returns change to a descriptor or policy different from the one that funded it, or when later co‑spends merge unrelated histories. Quarantine such change or re‑segregate it during low-fee windows.
