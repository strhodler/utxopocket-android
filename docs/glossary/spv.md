---
id: spv
title: Simplified Payment Verification (SPV)
summary: Lightweight verification model that trusts peers for inclusion proofs instead of fully validating blocks.
related: [node-trust-model, electrum-servers]
keywords: [spv, trust, validation]
---

SPV clients verify headers and rely on peers for transaction proofs, trading stronger trust assumptions for lower resource usage. Running your own backend over Tor improves privacy versus public servers.
