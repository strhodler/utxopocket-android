---
id: vbytes
title: Virtual bytes (vbytes)
summary: Size unit used for fee calculation; equals transaction weight divided by four, rounded up.
related: [fee-selection-playbook, rbf-cpfp-strategies]
keywords: [fees, weight, vbytes]
---

Fee rates are quoted in sats/vbyte. Understanding vbytes helps estimate costs and choose appropriate RBF or CPFP strategies without overpaying.
