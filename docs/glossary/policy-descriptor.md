---
id: policy-descriptor
title: Policy descriptor
summary: A descriptor that encodes a spending policy (often via Miniscript) and deterministically maps to scripts and addresses.
related: [descriptors-advanced]
keywords: [descriptor, policy, miniscript]
---

Policy descriptors describe “who can spend and when” using composable conditions. They allow wallets to analyze, derive, and validate the resulting scripts consistently across implementations.
