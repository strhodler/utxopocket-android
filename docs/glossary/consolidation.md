title: Consolidation
summary: Merging smaller UTXOs into larger ones to reduce future fees; risky if it links compartments.
related: [consolidation-strategy]
keywords: [fees, privacy]
---

Consolidation builds larger UTXOs from many small ones to cut future fees. Keep consolidations within a single descriptor/bucket and script family, and schedule them during low-fee windows to limit cost and fingerprints.
