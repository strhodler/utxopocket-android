---
id: multisig
title: Multisig (M‑of‑N)
summary: A spending policy requiring M of N keys to authorize a transaction.
related: [descriptors-advanced]
keywords: [multisig, policy, security]
---

Multisig policies are expressed in descriptors (often via `wsh(sortedmulti(...))` or Miniscript). PSBTs coordinate cosigners while keeping keys on their respective devices.
