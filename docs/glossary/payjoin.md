---
id: payjoin
title: PayJoin (BIP‑78)
summary: An interactive protocol where the receiver adds inputs, obscuring ownership and amounts.
related: [rbf-cpfp-strategies]
keywords: [privacy, interaction]
---

PayJoin breaks simple ownership assumptions by having both sender and receiver contribute inputs to a payment. It hides the payment amount and weakens clustering. Watch‑only users can prepare labels and candidate inputs; a compatible signer performs the interactive step.
