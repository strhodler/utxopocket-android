title: Non‑KYC
summary: Acquisition methods that do not require linking legal identity to UTXOs.
related: [non-kyc-acquisition]
keywords: [privacy, sourcing]
---

Non‑KYC refers to obtaining bitcoin without submitting identity documents to a custodian. It reduces linkability between you and specific UTXOs when paired with good segregation and labeling practices.
