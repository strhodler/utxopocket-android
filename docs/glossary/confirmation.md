---
id: confirmation
title: Confirmation
summary: Each block added on top of a transaction increases its security against reorgs and double‑spends.
related: [confirmation-policy]
keywords: [finality, depth]
---

A confirmation is counted when a block that includes your transaction is mined, and increases by one for each subsequent block on the chain tip. More confirmations mean higher reorg resistance. Choose thresholds per use‑case; see confirmation policy guidance.
