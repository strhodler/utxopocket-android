---
id: key-origin
title: Key origin (BIP32)
summary: The pair of master key fingerprint and derivation path that identifies where an xpub or key was derived from.
related: [descriptor-maps-and-recovery, descriptors-advanced]
keywords: [origin, bip32, fingerprint]
---

Including key origin data in descriptors and PSBTs prevents accidental mixups between accounts or branches, making restores and cosigner coordination reproducible.
