---
id: cosigner
title: Cosigner
summary: A participant who holds one key in a multisig policy and contributes signatures to PSBTs.
related: [descriptor-maps-and-recovery, descriptors-advanced]
keywords: [cosigner, multisig, psbt]
---

Cosigners are identified by key fingerprints and origin paths in descriptors and PSBTs, ensuring each device signs for the intended branch without key mixups.
