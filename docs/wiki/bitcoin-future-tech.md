## Evaluate by impact, not buzz
New proposals should be judged by security, privacy, interoperability, and operational complexity. A feature is useful only if it improves your actual workflow.

## Themes to watch
- Better policy expression and portability.
- Improved transaction efficiency and script privacy.
- Stronger interoperability through explicit descriptors and metadata.

## Watch-only takeaway
Even when signing features evolve, watch-only monitoring remains valuable: policy review, spend simulation, and independent verification become more important over time.

## Action checklist
- [ ] Track standards maturity before adopting new patterns.
- [ ] Prefer changes with clear recovery and migration paths.
- [ ] Document assumptions in team policy notes.
