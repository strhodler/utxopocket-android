## Consistency beats heroics
Most failures come from rushed decisions and incomplete records. Small, repeatable routines prevent expensive mistakes.

## Core habits
- Keep labels and backups current.
- Review destination and change policy before signing.
- Verify sync health after connectivity incidents.
- Practice recovery drills before emergencies.

## Privacy tie-in
Operational hygiene is privacy hygiene: disciplined behavior reduces accidental linkage and metadata leaks.

## Action checklist
- [ ] Maintain a short pre-send checklist.
- [ ] Schedule periodic backup restore tests.
- [ ] Audit labels and stale compartments monthly.
